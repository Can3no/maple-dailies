About The Site
Hi there! I made this app because I was getting frustrated having to maintain 2-3 spreadsheets, along with countless lists trying to track my progression in Maple. I tried a bunch of different options, one of which was setting up a Notion page and while that lasted me a good while, I eventually ended up deciding to develop my own solution!
I wanted this app to mainly focus on easy access to everything I need to do in Maple; generally Arcane River dailies and bossing. I also wanted to include information I always revisit, like Muto recipes, training maps, and which Arcane dailies to always re-roll.
I'm definitely open to suggestions and constructive criticism! Just reach out to me either in Maple (in Bera, either Beefies or Bøscø), on discord at vzhny#3079, or create a Github issue.
